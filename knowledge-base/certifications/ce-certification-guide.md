# CE Certification Guide for HousePlus Products

## Overview
HousePlus products comply with CE (Conformité Européenne) directives for entering the European Economic Area (EEA) market. CE marking is self-declaration based on internal testing and third-party laboratory reports.

## Applicable CE Directives

| Product Category | Directive | Standard | Test Lab |
|----------------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Solar Panels | LVD (2014/35/EU), EMC (2014/30/EU) | IEC 61215, IEC 61730 | SGS / TÜV Rheinland |
| Solar Inverters | LVD, EMC, RoHS (2011/65/EU) | IEC 62109-1 | TÜV Rheinland |
| LiFePO4 Batteries | LVD, EMC, RoHS | IEC 62619 | SGS |
| Home Appliances | LVD, EMC, RoHS, ErP | EN 60335-1 | Intertek |
| 3C Electronics | EMC, LVD, RoHS | EN 55032, EN 55035 | SGS |

## CE Technical File Contents
- Product description and photos
- Circuit diagrams and BOM
- Risk assessment report
- Test reports from accredited lab
- User manual (EN/FR/DE/ES)
- EU Declaration of Conformity (DoC)
- QC inspection records (ISO 9001)

## DoC Template (HousePlus)
```
EU DECLARATION OF CONFORMITY
Manufacturer: HousePlus Group Limited
Product: [Product Name / Model]
Directives: 2014/35/EU (LVD), 2014/30/EU (EMC)
Standards: [List IEC/EN standards]
Year of issue: [YYYY]
Signature: ________________
```

## CE Marking Requirements for Wholesale Buyers
- CE mark must be visible on product label and packaging
- DoC must be provided with shipment
- Technical file must be kept for 10 years
- Importers in EU must retain technical file and register with national authority

## Target Markets Requiring CE
- European Union (27 countries)
- United Kingdom (UKCA also required post-Brexit)
- Turkey, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein
- Many African and Asian markets accept CE as equivalent to local standards

## HousePlus CE Compliance Process
1. Pre-production sample submitted to SGS / TÜV
2. Lab testing per applicable IEC/EN standards
3. Internal QC based on ISO 9001:2015 procedures
4. EU DoC signed by HousePlus technical director
5. CE mark printed on product label and packaging
6. Technical file archived for 10 years
