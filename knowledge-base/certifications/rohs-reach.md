# RoHS & REACH Compliance Guide for HousePlus Products

## RoHS 2 Directive (2011/65/EU)

RoHS (Restriction of Hazardous Substances) restricts the use of 10 hazardous substances in electrical and electronic equipment.

### Restricted Substances (Max Concentration)

| Substance | Limit (by weight) |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Lead (Pb) | 0.1% (1000 ppm) |
| Mercury (Hg) | 0.1% |
| Cadmium (Cd) | 0.01% (100 ppm) |
| Hexavalent chromium (Cr6+) | 0.1% |
| PBB (Polybrominated biphenyls) | 0.1% |
| PBDE (Polybrominated diphenyl ethers) | 0.1% |
| DEHP (Bis(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate) | 0.1% |
| BBP (Butyl benzyl phthalate) | 0.1% |
| DBP (Dibutyl phthalate) | 0.1% |
| DIBP (Diisobutyl phthalate) | 0.1% |

### HousePlus RoHS Compliance Process
1. Raw material suppliers provide RoHS compliance certificates (SGS/Intertek)
2. Incoming material inspection (spot test with XRF analyzer)
3. Finished product third-party lab testing (SGS or TÜV)
4. RoHS Declaration of Conformity issued
5. RoHS mark printed on product label and packaging

### Target Markets (Mandatory)
- European Union (EU 27)
- United Kingdom (UK RoHS)
- China (China RoHS 2)
- Turkey, Switzerland

## REACH Regulation (EC 1907/2006)

REACH (Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals) applies to all chemical substances in products.

### Key Requirements for HousePlus Products
- SVHC (Substances of Very High Concern) disclosure if >0.1% by weight
- Current SVHC candidate list (Jan 2026): 247 substances
- SCIP database notification for articles containing SVHC >0.1%
- SDS (Safety Data Sheet) for chemicals used in manufacturing

### HousePlus High-Risk Components (SVHC Check Required)
| Component | Potential SVHC | Mitigation |
|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| PVC cables | Phthalates (DEHP, DBP) | Use phthalate-free PVC |
| Solder | Lead (Pb) | Use lead-free solder (RoHS compliant) |
| PCB laminate | Halogenated flame retardants | Use halogen-free laminate |
| Battery electrolyte | Cobalt salts | Source from certified suppliers |

### REACH Compliance Flow
1. BOM (Bill of Materials) review for SVHC (247 substances)
2. Supplier declarations for all raw materials
3. Third-party SVHC screening test (SGS)
4. SCIP database notification (if SVHC >0.1%)
5. SDS prepared for each product
6. EU importer notified of REACH obligations

### Target Markets
- European Union (mandatory)
-Importers in Germany, France, Netherlands must hold technical file
- South Korea (K-REACH), but China (China REACH not yet enforced)

## Documentation for Wholesale Buyers
When exporting to EU, HousePlus provides:
- RoHS Declaration of Conformity (per product model)
- REACH SVHC compliance statement
- SGS test report (RoHS 10 substances + SVHC screening)
- SDS (Safety Data Sheet) for batteries and chemicals
- Technical file retained for 10 years (available upon authority request)
