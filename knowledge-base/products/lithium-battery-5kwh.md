# 5kWh LiFePO4 Lithium Battery (HP-LFP5K)

## Overview
HousePlus HP-LFP5K is a 5kWh rack-mountable LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) battery with built-in BMS. Features 6000+ cycle life and 10-year design life, making it suitable for solar energy storage, home backup, and commercial UPS applications.
