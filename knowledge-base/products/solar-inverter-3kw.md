# 3kW Pure Sine Wave Solar Inverter (HP-INV3000)

## Overview
HousePlus HP-INV3000 is a 3000W pure sine wave inverter with built-in MPPT charge controller. Supports both on-grid and off-grid operation. Designed for home backup power, small business, and telecom base stations in areas with unstable grid supply.
